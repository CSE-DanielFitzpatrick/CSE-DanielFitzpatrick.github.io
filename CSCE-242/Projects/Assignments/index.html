Sharks

Sharks are considered to be some of the feircest predetors in the ocean. Their are around 520 described species of sharks. They are generally considered to be carnivors, which means that their diets consist of mostly meat. Sharks are fish that use their gills to breathe.

More on Different Species of Sharks

Though many people fear sharks it is a common misconception that sharks try to harm humans. The reason people are attcked is because the sharks confuse them for other sea animals that they often eat. The Atlantic ocean has the most recorded shark attacks. The waters off the coast of Florida are heavily infested with sharks.
