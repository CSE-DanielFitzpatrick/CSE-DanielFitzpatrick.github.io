About Us

Dinner and A Movie is a premier entertainment venue that combines the joy of dining with the excitement of cinema. Offering a unique experience, guests can enjoy delicious, chef-inspired meals while watching the latest blockbuster films in the comfort of a luxurious theater setting. Whether you're visiting for a casual night out, a special occasion, or hosting a private event, Dinner and A Movie provides a memorable experience with exceptional service, top-quality food, and state-of-the-art movie screenings. With customizable event packages, it's also the perfect place to rent out a theater for birthdays, corporate gatherings, or private parties.
